Who I am

Web Developer based out of San Diego, CA

In 2017, I left my 9-5 desk job to pursue freelance work in photography and videography. After working with various companies, brands, and building an instagram page with over 20,000 followers, I decided to look toward another direction in life...

During the locksdowns of the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic, I wasn't able to work during that time and I had to stay inside. It got me thinking a lot about my career and where I wanted to go. Ultimately after several months of thinking and researching, I decided to dive head first into the world of tech. I used to do projects back in high school and college with HTMl, CSS and some wordpress. But I never got serious with it until August 2020.

From there I decided to take various courses using Team Treehouse, Harvard CS50x, The Odin Project, Scrimba, and Full Stack open from the Univeristy of Helsinki. Ever since then, theres been no turning back and I have been on the a journey to become a full time Front End Web Developer!
