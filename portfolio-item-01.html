ASU Prep Digital - The Learning Cloud

A React/Typescript App

ASU Prep Digital's - The Learning Cloud is a proprietary new platform allowing digital content to integrate seamlessly into any LMS, while supporting key features that engage and personalize learning for every student.

I worked on maintaing and enhancing the Course Designer, Student Experience, and Teacher Experience.

The Course Designer is an application that teachers can use to design their own custom courses for their students. When the teachers are ready publish the course, it can be seen on the Student Experience. Lastly, the Teacher Experience is an application that teachers can use to organize their courses and view their student's progress.

Technologies used: Typescript/React, Nodejs, and MongoDB
